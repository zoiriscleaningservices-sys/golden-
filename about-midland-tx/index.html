Founded on Trust, Grown by Consistency

Golden Grove Cleaning started with a simple observation: Midland deserved a cleaning service that combined the reliability of a large franchise with the personal touch of a neighbor.

What began as a small family operation has grown into Midland's most trusted name in professional cleaning. We've built our reputation on one house, one office, and one successful clean at a time.

Our mission is simple: to give our clients their time back. In the fast-paced energy of the Permian Basin, a clean home is a sanctuary. We're here to ensure you have that sanctuary without the stress.
